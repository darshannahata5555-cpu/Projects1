PANCAKES

INGRIDIENTS

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour (atta)
  • 2 medium potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • ½ tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp coriander leaves (chopped)
  • Ghee or oil for cooking

Steps

  • In a bowl, mix wheat flour with water and knead into a soft dough. Rest it for 15–20 minutes.
  • In another bowl, combine mashed potatoes, onions, chilies, and all spices. Mix well to make stuffing.
  • Take a small portion of dough and roll it into a small circle.
  • Place 1–2 tbsp of the stuffing in the center and seal the edges to make a ball.
  • Gently roll again into a round paratha.
  • Heat a tawa or pan and cook the paratha on both sides using ghee or oil until golden brown.
  • Serve hot with curd, pickle, or butter!
HAVE FUN!
